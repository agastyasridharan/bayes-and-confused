# Query Templates (Exoplanet)

Frozen before trajectory collection. Each template takes `{pl_name}` as input.
5 templates per property × 4 properties = 20 total.

## pl_orbper

1. "What is the orbital period of {pl_name}?"
2. "Look up the orbital period for {pl_name} in the NASA Exoplanet Archive."
3. "Can you find the orbital period (in days) for the planet {pl_name}?"
4. "I need the orbital period of {pl_name}. Please query the exoplanet archive."
5. "Report the orbital period of {pl_name} from available exoplanet data."
